Privacy Policy for SmsTask

Last updated: December 9, 2024

1. Information We Collect

This application does NOT collect any personal information. The app only:

  • Sends SMS messages to numbers you explicitly specify
  • Stores server URL configuration locally on your device
  • Logs message sending status for debugging purposes

2. SMS Permission Usage

The SEND_SMS permission is required to:

  • Send SMS messages programmatically through your device
  • Select specific SIM card when sending messages (dual-SIM devices)
  • Report SMS sending status back to your configured server

We do NOT:

  • Read your incoming SMS messages
  • Access your SMS inbox
  • Store your SMS content on external servers
  • Send SMS without your explicit action

3. Data Storage

All data (server URL, pending messages) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to third parties.
